Established in 1991 in Greece, Road Runner Bus Lines is a trusted provider of safe and reliable bus transportation services. With a modern fleet of buses and experienced drivers, the company has become a preferred choice for travelers looking to explore the country in comfort and style. Despite facing challenges, Road Runner Bus Lines remains committed to its core values of safety, reliability, and customer service, and looks forward to serving customers for years to come.

Booking Methods:
At Road Runners, we offer convenient and easy-to-use booking options to suit your needs.
You can book your tickets through any of the following methods:
Online Booking: You can book your tickets online through our website or mobile app. Our online booking system is fast, secure, and easy to use. You can choose your seat, make payment, and receive your e-ticket in just a few clicks.
Phone Booking: If you prefer to book your tickets over the phone, our customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you. Simply call our booking hotline and provide your travel details, and our agents will guide you through the booking process and help you secure your seats.
In-person Booking: For those who prefer to book their tickets in person, you can visit any of our ticketing offices located in major cities across the country. Our friendly staff will be happy to assist you with your booking and answer any questions you may have about our services.
No matter which booking method you choose, we guarantee a hassle-free and enjoyable booking experience. So why wait? Book your tickets with Road Runners today and experience the best in bus travel.

Refunds and Cancellations:

  • Refunds available for cancelled tickets, subject to conditions.
  • Full refund for cancellation made over 24 hrs before scheduled departure time.
  • Cancellation fee of 10% for cancellation within 24 hrs of scheduled departure time.
  • No refunds for tickets cancelled after scheduled departure time.
  • Road Runners may cancel or reschedule trip due to unforeseen circumstances.
  • Missed departure time treated as cancellation with no refund.
  • Contact 24/7 customer support for assistance.
Baggage Policy
At Road Runners, we want to make your travel experience as comfortable as possible. We allow each passenger to bring one carry-on bag and one personal item, such as a purse or laptop bag, on board free of charge.
Your carry-on bag must fit in the overhead compartment or under the seat in front of you, and must not exceed the following dimensions: 22 x 14 x 9 inches (56 x 36 x 23 cm). Your personal item must fit under the seat in front of you and should not exceed the dimensions of 18 x 14 x 8 inches (46 x 36 x 20 cm).
brFor checked baggage, we allow up to two pieces of luggage per passenger, with a maximum weight of 50 pounds (23 kg) per bag. Any additional bags or bags exceeding the weight limit may be subject to additional fees.
Certain items are not allowed on board, such as hazardous materials, explosives, or live animals (with the exception of service animals). If you have any questions about our baggage policy or the items you can bring on board, please contact our customer support team.
